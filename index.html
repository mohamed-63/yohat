About YOHAT

YOHAT is a company specialized in the extraction, processing and marketing of 100% Algerian natural stones.

We offer a wide range of materials for construction, interior design and architecture: marble, granite, travertine, sandstone and onyx.

With full control of the production chain, YOHAT guarantees high-quality products that meet the technical and aesthetic requirements of both professionals and private customers.
